Project1- EVL - Sweet Home Chicago

Interesting Findings

One of the major objectives of creating this application was to enable the user to find and unearth various hidden patterns across the city of Chicago.
Below are some of my findings.

  • There is a sizable number of people of Mexican Descent. In some communities they are even greater than African American Community
  • Asian Population is majorly concentrated across some of the community areas such as Armour Square.
  • There are a lot more people from South American Countries as compared to European Countries

Chicago has been categorized as a melting pot for multiple communities and culture and after development and creation of my application, I can attest to this fact. Exploring Chicago by traversing it through the different types of data has been eye opening and enlightening.
